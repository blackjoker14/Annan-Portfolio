About Geronimo Siltion

Geronimo Stilton is an Italian children's book series written by Elisabetta Dami (though the covers claim they were written by the title character). ... A best-selling author, Geronimo Stilton works as a journalist and editor for the fictional newspaper The Rodent's Gazette.

Who is Geronimo Stiltion book author and about it ?

Elisabetta Dami

The original Italian publisher, Edizioni Piemme, insisted on supporting the idea that many kids had about Geronimo and his adventures being real. It was later revealed that Elisabetta Dami, an Italian author, world traveller, and co-owner of the company that publishes Geronimo's books, was also his creator. She was born 1958, Milan, Italy.
